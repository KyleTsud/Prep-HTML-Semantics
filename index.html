Oahu

Febuary 2015

Three surfers glide down the face of a 12 foot(hawaiian scale) tall wave. Located on Oahu's North Shore, this dangerous wave can be found roughly a half mile out from the shore line. For expert level surfers only. One should only venture out here if they can, hold their breath for 3 minutes, be in top physical shape, and have the right equipment.

Himalayas was once a surf spot known to only a handful of local surfers but with the advent of surf media the once closely held surf spot is now a google search away.
